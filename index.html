10 Commandments

  • Keep as much of your logic in your block as possible. This makes the logic reusable and allows the delegation of templating tasks to less experienced members of staff.
  • Translate all strings.
  • Use Lazy loading
  • Use events system where possible. Avoid rewrites
  • Don't var_dump
  • Use MAGE_IS_DEVELOPER_MODE
  • Check third party extensions for model rewrites and extend into local module
  • Don't commit commented out code
